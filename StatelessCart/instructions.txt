
Instructions for application Stateless Cart

Create a e-commerce site consisting of at least three pages:

+ Display at least five items that can be purchased along with a quantity field
  and price for each. Provide a submit button that sends the order to the server
  for processing.

+ Create an order summary page based on the quantities chosen on the first page.
  Display a subtotal for confirmation. When the user confirms the purchase they
  are sent to the final page for payment.

+ Calculate the tax based on the rate in Florence. Display the subtotal, tax,
  and total.
